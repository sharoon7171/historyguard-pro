HistoryGuard Pro

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 6, 2025

Privacy First: HistoryGuard Pro is designed with privacy as the core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All functionality operates locally on your device.

1. Information We Access

HistoryGuard Pro requires access to the following data to provide its core functionality:

1.1 Browser History Data

  • Purpose: To monitor new history entries and delete existing entries based on your keyword filters
  • Usage: When privacy protection is enabled, we block new history entries from being saved. When keyword filtering is active, we delete history entries that match your specified keywords
  • Storage: We do not store or save any history data
  • Transmission: No history data is transmitted to external servers

1.2 Extension Settings

  • Purpose: To save your privacy protection toggle state and keyword filters
  • Data Types: Boolean values (enabled/disabled), text strings (keywords), timestamps
  • Storage: Stored locally using Chrome's secure storage API
  • Sync: Settings may sync across your Chrome browsers if you have Chrome sync enabled

2. What We Do NOT Collect

HistoryGuard Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, address)
  • Browsing content or page data
  • Passwords or authentication information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or medical information
  • Personal communications (emails, messages)
  • User activity tracking (clicks, keystrokes)

3. How We Use Your Data

Any data we access is used exclusively for the following purposes:

3.1 History Management

  • Monitor new history entries in real-time
  • Block new entries when privacy protection is enabled
  • Delete existing entries that match your keyword filters
  • Provide visual feedback on the extension's status

3.2 Settings Persistence

  • Remember your privacy protection preference
  • Store your custom keyword filters
  • Maintain settings across browser sessions
  • Enable settings synchronization across devices (if Chrome sync is enabled)

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage Only

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

4.2 Chrome Sync

If you have Chrome sync enabled, your extension settings may be synchronized across your Chrome browsers. This synchronization is handled entirely by Chrome's built-in sync system and is subject to Chrome's privacy policies.

4.3 Data Encryption

All stored data is encrypted by Chrome's security system and is only accessible to the HistoryGuard Pro extension.

5. Third-Party Services

HistoryGuard Pro does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking systems. The extension operates entirely independently and does not communicate with external servers.

6. Your Rights and Control

6.1 Complete Control

  • You can enable or disable privacy protection at any time
  • You can add, modify, or remove keyword filters at any time
  • You can uninstall the extension at any time, which will remove all stored settings
  • You have full control over what gets blocked or deleted

6.2 Data Deletion

To delete all data associated with HistoryGuard Pro:

  • Uninstall the extension from Chrome
  • Clear the extension's data from Chrome's extension management page
  • All settings and preferences will be permanently removed

7. Children's Privacy

HistoryGuard Pro does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The extension is designed for general use and does not require age verification.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically.

9. Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • Chrome Extension Manifest V3 requirements
  • General privacy best practices for browser extensions
